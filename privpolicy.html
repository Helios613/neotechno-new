Privacy Policy

NeoTechnoSoft’s Privacy Policy

We at NeoTechnoSoft are committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and secure online experience. You can go through our site without giving us any information about yourself. However, sometimes we may need your information to provide the services you request, and this statement of privacy explains NeoTechnoSoft‘s data collection and use practices in those situations.

Collecting Your Personal Information

We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you (personal information) or allows us to contact you. This information is limited to your name, e-mail address, telephone number, company affiliation, country, or location. However, it may include other necessary information to provide your requested service. We collect information via the following modes or platforms:
1. When you visit the Websites owned by NeoTechnoSoft.
2. When you download apps developed by NeoTechnoSoft from Google Play Store or Apple Store. These apps are used for delivery of services to you.
3. When you interact with us on social platforms like LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, and Google.
4. When you interact on communication platforms like WhatsApp for Business, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet/Duo, and others.

Use Of Your Personal Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:
1. Make your website visit, marketing experience, and communications tailored to your interests based on your personal information.
2. Deliver support and services that you request or purchase.
3. Evaluate and improve our products, services, marketing, and customer relationships.
4. Interact with you on the third-party social networks. Our interactions with you on a third-party social network would be subject to the respective network’s privacy policies and terms of use.
5. Alert you of product upgrades, exclusive offers, updated information, and other new services from NeoTechnoSoft.
6. Communicate with you about conferences, workshops or any other events hosted or co-hosted by NeoTechnoSoft or one of our partners.

Security of your Personal Information

We strictly protect the security of your personal information and honor your choices for its intended use.

Changes to this Statement

We will occasionally update this privacy statement. When we do, we will also revise the “last updated” date at the top of the privacy statement. Kindly check this page regularly to review if any changes are introduced.
